What is Marriage Matching?

Marriage matching, or Kundli Milan, is the Vedic practice of comparing birth charts of prospective bride and groom to assess compatibility. This ancient science helps predict marital harmony, longevity, and prosperity of the union.

Factors We Analyze

  • Guna Milan: 36-point compatibility system
  • Manglik Dosha: Mars affliction analysis
  • Nadi Dosha: Health and progeny concerns
  • Bhakoot Dosha: Financial and family harmony
  • 7th House: Marriage house examination
  • Venus & Mars: Love and passion indicators

Compatibility Areas

  • Emotional and mental compatibility
  • Physical attraction and intimacy
  • Financial stability and prosperity
  • Family harmony and relationships
  • Children and progeny prospects
  • Longevity of marriage
  • Overall happiness and fulfillment

Our Matching Process

We provide comprehensive Kundli matching reports with detailed analysis of all compatibility factors. If doshas are found, we suggest effective remedies. Our goal is to help you make informed decisions for a happy, harmonious married life.
