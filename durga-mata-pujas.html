About Goddess Durga

Goddess Durga is the supreme mother goddess who destroys evil and protects devotees. She grants prosperity, success, courage, and fulfillment of wishes. Her blessings bring abundance, victory, and spiritual growth.

Types of Durga Pujas

  • Durga Puja: Complete worship ceremony
  • Durga Homa: Fire ritual for blessings
  • Durga Saptashati Path: Sacred text recitation
  • Durga Yantra Puja: Sacred geometry worship
  • Durga Abhishek: Sacred bathing ritual
  • Navratri Puja: Nine-day festival worship

Benefits of Durga Puja

  • Victory over enemies and obstacles
  • Prosperity and wealth accumulation
  • Success in endeavors
  • Protection from harm
  • Fulfillment of wishes
  • Family harmony and happiness
  • Spiritual growth and enlightenment
