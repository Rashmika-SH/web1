What is Horoscope Reading?

Horoscope reading is the interpretation of celestial movements and their influence on human affairs. Based on your birth chart and current planetary transits, we provide detailed forecasts for various aspects of your life including career, relationships, health, and finances.

Types of Horoscope Readings

  • Daily Horoscope: Day-to-day guidance and predictions
  • Weekly Forecast: Seven-day outlook and planning
  • Monthly Reading: Comprehensive monthly predictions
  • Yearly Forecast: Annual overview and major events
  • Transit Analysis: Current planetary influences
  • Solar Return: Birthday year predictions
