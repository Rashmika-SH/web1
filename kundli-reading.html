What is Kundli Reading?

Kundli, also known as birth chart or horoscope, is a detailed astrological chart based on your exact birth time, date, and place. It maps the positions of planets and celestial bodies at the moment of your birth, revealing your karmic blueprint and life path.

Kundli Components We Analyze

  • 12 Houses: Different life areas and experiences
  • 9 Planets: Planetary influences and energies
  • 27 Nakshatras: Lunar mansions and characteristics
  • Yogas: Planetary combinations and their effects
  • Doshas: Malefic influences and remedies
  • Dasha Periods: Planetary time cycles

Life Areas Covered

  • Career prospects and professional success
  • Marriage compatibility and relationship harmony
  • Health patterns and wellness guidance
  • Financial stability and wealth accumulation
  • Education and learning opportunities
  • Family relationships and children
  • Spiritual growth and moksha path
